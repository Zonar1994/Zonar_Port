Introduction

You can read about my projects and experiences, offering a straightforward look at what I have learned and accomplished.

This structured approach has guided my process and ensured a thorough exploration and realization of ideas. This also serves as the structure for this website.

The Plan

Research and Methodology

Since this is going to be an explorative and in-depth project, I chose to do the Creative Technology Internship in order to dive into this domain and explore the possibilities within that specific domain. Creative Tech allows me to build and experiment with different technologies and also research those technologies. It is based on triggers that start the project and that will slowly turn into a complete concept. Next to that, it will also have a prototype that shows how the concept would work in a way—not a fully working prototype but a proof of concept. Together with my vision, what started all of this, and the vision of Henhouse, I aim to use the Creative Tech methodology in order to dive into the world of AI within organizations. This methodology includes phases such as the Trigger Phase, Ideation Phase, Specification & Design, Realisation, and Evaluation.

Strategy and Execution

Embracing the Scrum methodology for daily organization and prioritizing flexibility, I will dedicate myself to this project, exploring the transformative potential of AI.


The phases of the Creative Technology Methodology connect in a seamless workflow that ensures comprehensive project development. The Trigger Phase and Ideation Phase fall within the IDEA stage, where initial motivations and brainstorming are consolidated into actionable concepts. The Ideation and Specification & Design phases bridge into the CONCEPT stage, focusing on refining ideas into detailed plans. The Specification & Design and Realisation phases sit within the DESIGN stage, where concrete plans are turned into functional prototypes. Finally, the Realisation and Evaluation phases comprise the PRODUCT stage, where the developed solutions are implemented, tested, and refined based on user feedback.

This structured approach allows for iterative development and continuous improvement, ensuring that each phase builds upon the previous one. During these phases, I will be engaging in various activities such as brainstorming, design documentation, system setup, and user testing to ensure that the project evolves effectively from initial concept to final product.
