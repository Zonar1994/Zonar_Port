Discover Phase

Introduction

As I begin the Discover Phase of my internship project, I will conduct a detailed examination of generative AI. The main goal is to assess its potential to enhance workplace practices, boost creativity, and increase productivity. This initial phase focuses on comprehensive research, systematic analysis, and strategic investigation of how AI can meet our organization's unique needs and methodologies.

I will start the Discover Phase with an extensive literature review to build a theoretical basis for the project, drawing on recent academic progress in AI technology and its applications in work processes similar to ours. Additionally, I will conduct interviews with industry experts to gain insights into the successful use of AI and any associated challenges. At the same time, I will experiment with various AI platforms and tools to understand their capabilities in our specific work environment.

We have identified the need for a system that integrates AI in our workplace operations. This involves using generative AI to create content and perform tasks based on available information and self-devised strategies, tailored to our users/employees' needs.
Our strategy is divided into four main areas: suggestions of elements, reverse engineering, personas, and task execution.




Research Questions

  1. AI-Driven Organizational Transformation: How can AI tools be applied to organizations to enhance the workflow and also make it more efficient?
  2. AI's Influence on Workplace Dynamics: What changes might AI bring to our team dynamics, roles, and the overall work culture?
  3. Innovative and Strategic Applications of AI: In what practical ways can AI aid in problem-solving and decision-making within an organization?
  4. Societal and Ethical Considerations: What ethical guidelines should be considered when implementing AI, to ensure it supports our values and respects employee’s?
  5. Measuring AI's Organizational Impact: How will I measure the effectiveness of AI in improving our workflow and output quality?
  6. Future-Proofing and Sustainable AI Integration: What steps can we take to ensure our use of AI remains adaptable and responsible as technology evolves?




Current Situation

At Henhouse Studio, we've completely changed how we manage projects and stay productive by using tools like Notion and n8n. We love that it has tables for detailed stuff, and boards for seeing the big picture. This means everyone can track projects the way that works best for them.


n8n is amazing because it connects Notion to tons of other apps and services we use, automating a lot of the boring tasks. It even has AI features now, which help us make decisions and analyze information faster. This saves our creative team a bunch of time, so they can focus on actually being creative!


Basically, Notion and n8n make us work smarter, faster, and better. We're always looking for ways to improve, and these tools help us do exactly that. We're excited to see where this takes us next!

  • Enhanced Project Management: Notion provides a dual-view system to accommodate different project management styles, balancing detailed task oversight with high-level progress visualization.

  • Workflow Automation: n8n introduces powerful automation capabilities, seamlessly integrating with Notion and other APIs to streamline data processes and reduce manual tasks.
  • AI-Enhanced Processes: The integration of AI nodes via n8n allows for the automation of complex decision-making tasks, enhancing efficiency and precision.
  • Innovative Technological Synergy: The combination of Notion and n8n places Henhouse Studio at the forefront of technological integration, fostering an innovative environment for creative industries.

Literature Study: Enhancing organizational Effecientcy and Creativity

To lay a strong foundation for my AI integration project, I embarked on a comprehensive literature study exploring the complex ways AI influences organizational effectiveness. My goal was to understand how AI could optimize operations, improve team collaboration, and enhance my strategic decision-making. I focused on understanding practical applications, cultural implications, and the ethical considerations surrounding AI in the workplace.

My study involved analyzing case studies for real-world examples of AI successes and challenges, examining the interplay between AI and human judgment, and exploring AI's potential in human resources to boost employee performance and adaptability.
The results were insightful. They demonstrated AI's potential to drive organizational innovation when implemented with a clear strategy and in harmony with human creativity and values. It became evident that AI solutions must be rooted in ethics and prioritize human-centric design to augment work rather than replace it.

This research concluded that successful AI integration demands careful planning and ethical awareness. It provided me with a framework for applying AI that not only streamlines current processes but also future-proofs my organization. This study revealed the importance of approaching AI as a tool to drive growth, foster continuous improvement, and achieve sustainable success.

  • Strategic Deployment: The study highlighted the importance of a strategic approach to AI integration, ensuring that the technology is not simply added, but intricately woven into organizational processes to maximize value and efficiency.
  • Human-AI Symbiosis: It was revealed that the true value of AI lies in its ability to complement human intelligence, fostering a collaborative workspace where human creativity is enhanced by AI's data processing and analytical capabilities.
  • Ethical and Human-Centric Approach: The research emphasized the need for AI implementations to be guided by ethical considerations and a human-centric design philosophy, ensuring that technology serves to support and elevate human labor.

Existing Concepts

Suggestions of Elements

This involves generating suggestions to capture all aspects of specific elements, such as a brand guide. What does it consist of? This feature aids in understanding various components and enriching datasets within the company.

Reverse Engineering

This allows the system to dissect a product, value stream, or project comprehensively. Taking the brand guide as an example again, the system will break down the entire guide into actionable steps on such a granular level that anyone can follow. This process will identify tasks that require human intervention, which employees will undertake, allowing the AI to continue with the dissection effectively.

Personas

Here, we aim to create specific types of personas to garner a wide range of perspectives for extensive user research. Instead of developing a single persona and exploring all aspects of the journey map, we generate every possible persona. This approach maximizes the insights gained on a product by covering every perspective.

Expert Interview

In this phase, roles are assigned, and tasks generated by the reverse engineering process are executed. The reverse engineering only outlines the steps/tasks/project/plan, which are then carried through.
