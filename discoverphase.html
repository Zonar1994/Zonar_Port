Dscover Phase

As I commence the Discover Phase of my internship project, I will undertake a comprehensive investigation into the field of generative AI. My core objective is to evaluate its potential to optimize workplace practices, foster creativity, and augment overall productivity. This initial phase prioritizes thorough research, methodical analysis, and strategic exploration of how AI might align with our organization's specific needs and work methodologies.

I will initiate the Discover Phase with an extensive literature review. This will establish a theoretical framework for the project, drawing upon the latest scholarly advancements in AI technology and its applications in workflows mirroring our own. To supplement this research, I will consult domain experts through structured interviews, gleaning valuable knowledge on the successful implementation of AI and any inherent challenges. Concurrently, I will engage in direct experimentation with various AI platforms and tools, developing a working understanding of their capabilities within our specific work context.

We've determined the need to develop a system that utilizes AI in the workplace. This can be achieved by employing generative AI to create content and execute tasks based on existing information and self-generated plans, tailored to our users/employees' needs.
Our approach is segmented into four key areas: suggestions of elements, reverse engineering, personas, and task execution.




Suggestions of Elements

This involves generating suggestions to capture all aspects of specific elements, such as a brand guide. What does it consist of? This feature aids in understanding various components and enriching datasets within the company.

Reverse Engineering

This allows the system to dissect a product, value stream, or project comprehensively. Taking the brand guide as an example again, the system will break down the entire guide into actionable steps on such a granular level that anyone can follow. This process will identify tasks that require human intervention, which employees will undertake, allowing the AI to continue with the dissection effectively.

Personas

Here, we aim to create specific types of personas to garner a wide range of perspectives for extensive user research. Instead of developing a single persona and exploring all aspects of the journey map, we generate every possible persona.
