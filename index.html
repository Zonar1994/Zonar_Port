Introduction


Welcome to my graduation portfolio. I'm Zonar Caldera, wrapping up my final year in IT-Media Design at Fontys, and this website is a snapshot of my journey through my Creative Technology graduation internship. Here, I've laid out my projects and experiences, offering a straightforward look at what I've learned and accomplished.

This is a reflection on the process, the hurdles I've encountered, and the insights I've gained. My time here has been about exploring the intersection of artificial intelligence, design, and how these can be harnessed to address real-world challenges. From personal projects to collaborative efforts, each section of this portfolio reveals a step in my journey toward understanding and applying AI in meaningful ways.

With a focus on my graduation project, I've delved into the practical application of AI for organizations, aiming to demonstrate the tangible benefits of AI-human collaboration. This work, done in partnership with Henhouse Studio, reflects my commitment to not just envision but also to enact the integration of AI within creative and operational workflows.









My Vision

My fascination with AI started long before this project, driven by a vision to harness its power to amplify human potential in the workplace. I dream of a future where technology and creativity merge to unlock new levels of productivity and work-life balance. At Henhouse Studio, a creative hub for innovation, I found the perfect ground to put this vision to the test, aiming to demonstrate AI as a collaborative partner in redefining the essence of creative work.









“Harness the power of AI to redefine human potential in the workplace, fostering a future where technology amplifies our capabilities. This could potentially lead to a transformative shift in work-life balance and productivity. This future envisions AI as a collaborative partner, augmenting human efforts and opening new avenues for creativity and efficiency.”








Project Context:

Henhouse Studio: A beacon of creativity, Henhouse Studio has always been at the forefront of conceptual innovation. Our journey together on this project is a testament to their commitment to pushing boundaries and embracing sustainable, forward-thinking practices.

Stakeholders: The heart of this project lies with its stakeholders - from the creative minds within Henhouse Studio to our valued business partners and the audiences we serve. Their engagement and insights have been pivotal in shaping the direction and impact of our work.

Context: The seed for this project was sown by Henhouse Studio's vision of streamlining organizational processes through AI. In a world clinging to outdated methods, we see a golden opportunity to demonstrate the efficiency and creativity AI can bring to the workplace, not by replacing humans but by enhancing our work and creativity.





My Approach and Journey

The Plan: Stepping into this project, I knew a clear roadmap was essential. My plan is not just about reaching a destination but about learning and adapting through each phase - from the initial research and problem definition to strategy formulation and execution.

Research and Methodology: Grounded in a mix of literature review, expert consultations, and practical validation, my approach has been both analytical and hands-on. The Double Diamond method will be guiding me through the complexities of AI integration, balancing creativity with critical thinking.

Strategy and Execution: Embracing the Scrum methodology for daily organization and prioritizing flexibility, I will dedicate myself to this project, exploring the transformative potential of AI. Through phases of discovery, definition, development, and delivery, I've navigated challenges and celebrated milestones, keeping a keen eye on our goals and the evolving landscape of AI in creative industries.




Reflections and Future Directions

As I compile my findings and prepare for the final presentation, I reflect on the journey and the profound learnings it has brought. This project has been a blend of challenges and triumphs, a testament to the power of collaboration between human creativity and artificial intelligence.

I have been striving to understand how technology can benefit humanity ever since I first encountered computers at a young age. While everyone recognizes the potential benefits of technology, the specific nature of future technological advancements and their exact benefits remain uncertain. Even with the latest developments in AI, our understanding of their impact and implications is limited. These tools, designed with the intention of benefiting us, raise questions: Will advancements in technology ultimately replace us, benefit us, or simply make us lazy?
This is what I aim to explore in my Graduation Project. I intend to discover concrete and creative ways in which the technology of artificial intelligence can benefit humanity, specifically focusing on the relationship between organizations and humans.
