Reflection

In my project, I aimed to answer several key research questions to understand the impact of generative AI on organizational workflows.

Research Questions and Answers:

  • Main Research Question: How can generative AI enhance workflow efficiency in organizations?

    • I have addressed this by demonstrating how generative AI can improve certain tasks for project managers, leading to significant efficiency gains.

  • Generative AI's Influence on Workplace Dynamics: What changes might generative AI introduce to team dynamics and overall work culture?

    • This was explored in the evaluation phase, showing how generative AI can take over repetitive tasks, allowing project managers to focus on more strategic activities.

  • Innovative and Strategic Applications of Generative AI: In what practical ways can generative AI support problem-solving and decision-making within an organization?

    • The project breakdown bot demonstrated how generative AI can assist in breaking down projects, thus aiding in project management and decision-making.

  • Societal and Ethical Considerations: What ethical guidelines are important when implementing generative AI, to ensure it upholds company values and respects the employees?

    • This question was not directly addressed in the current project scope but remains an important consideration for future work.

  • Measuring Generative AI's Organizational Impact: How will we measure the effectiveness of generative AI in improving our workflow and output quality?

    • In the evaluation, I measured how the project breakdown bot can significantly improve task management, which suggests a positive impact on workflow efficiency.

  • Future-Proofing and Sustainable Generative AI Integration: What steps can I take to ensure our use of generative AI remains adaptable and responsible as technology evolves?

    • The project breakdown bot is designed to be adaptable and can be integrated into any system, allowing for continuous improvements and sustainability. This is further elaborated in the Advice Document.
